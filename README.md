## How to Play
Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.

To play the game:
1. Open the `index.html` file in your web browser.
2. Click on an empty cell to place your mark (X or O).
3. The game will automatically switch turns between the players.
4. The game will declare a winner if there is one, or announce a draw if the board is filled without a winner.
5. Click the "Reset" button to start a new game.
